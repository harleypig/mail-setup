Let's get your email set up 📬

Phone, tablet, or computer — these steps work on any device.

There are two parts. Before the switch, save a copy of your old email. Then after the switch, set up your new email wherever you read it. You don't need to know anything techy — just follow along.

Part 1 · Do this now

Before the switch

While your old email still exists — before Friday, July 24.

Save your old emails 📦

Your old messages live in your current harleypig.com mailbox. When we switch over, that old mailbox goes away — so if there are emails you want to keep, download a copy of them first with Google Takeout, Google's own free backup tool. It bundles all your mail into one file you can hang onto.

⏰

This only works before the switch on Friday, July 24. Once we flip over, the old emails are gone for good and there's no getting them back. Please don't leave it to the last minute.

Do this on a computer, signed in to your harleypig.com account:

  1. Go to takeout.google.com. Check that the little picture in the top-right corner is your harleypig.com account — if it isn't, switch to it.
  2. Click Deselect all at the top. That clears everything so you only grab your email.
  3. Scroll down to Mail and check its box.
  4. Scroll to the very bottom and click Next step.
  5. Leave it set to Send download link via email and Export once, then click Create export.
  6. Google does the work and emails you a download link — this can take anywhere from a few minutes to a few hours. Click it, download the file, and keep it somewhere safe. 🎉

You'll get a .zip file — just hang onto it. After the switch, the “Want your old emails back?” guide down below walks you through opening it.

IMAP or POP3?

Some apps ask you to pick one. Here's the short version — when in doubt, pick IMAP.

📥 IMAP Pick this

Your mail lives on the server, so it looks the same on every device — your phone, a tablet, a laptop. Read it in one place, it's read everywhere. This is what you want.

📦 POP3

Only use this if your app doesn't offer IMAP. POP3 downloads mail onto one device and usually removes it from the server — so it won't show up anywhere else. Same server, but use port 995.

Step by step in the Gmail app

The Gmail app comes on most Android phones and can hold your harleypig.com email too. Here's the whole thing:

  1. Open the Gmail app. Tap your profile picture in the top-right corner.
  2. Tap Add another account.
  3. Choose Other — not Google.
  4. Type your full email address (you@harleypig.com) and tap Next.
  5. Choose Personal (IMAP), then Next.
  6. Type your password and tap Next.
  7. If it asks for an incoming or outgoing server, put in mail.harleypig.com (copy it from the boxes above) and tap Next.
  8. That's it — you're done! 🎉 Give it a minute and your mail will show up.

You can actually peek at this one early: as soon as you have your new password you can log in. It just won't send or receive real email until after the switch on Friday, July 24.
